Cyd La Luz Cyd lives on the border of Nancy Pelosi's and Jackie Speier's congressional districts. The geolocation feature of this website can't decide which congresswoman represents him either. He's written letters to both congresswomen and has gotten replies from both, so the mixed results of the geolocation must be a feature and not a bug. In the past Cyd has worked as an elementary school teacher, a radio journalist, and a statistical analyst among other occupations.

Samuel J. Tilden Few people in American history have been hurt more by a lack of government transparency than Samuel J. Tilden, who lost the election of 1876 despite winning the popular vote. The election was ultimately decided by a 15-member panel of Republicans and Democrats who negotiated that Rutherford B. Hayes would win, but only if Republicans promised to end Reconstruction in the South. We named Tilden in his honor.

About Hack Reactor

Hack Reactor is an intensive study program that teaches Javascript, HTML5/CSS, Ruby on Rails, and emerging web development frameworks such as Meteor and Angular JS. Students attend the program for twelve weeks, six days a week, for 11 hours a day from 9AM to 8PM; most students, including the members of the Tilden team, stay in the office to work well beyond these hours. Students come to Hack Reactor to work hard, learn from some of the best in the business, and build great applications. Official Website
